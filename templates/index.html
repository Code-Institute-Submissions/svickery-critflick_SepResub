What is CritFlick?


CritFlick is your one stop shop for all your needs in movie reviews. Except here, we don't let the so call professionals give the reviews. We leave that to you!

For many years we have seen great movies get called all sorts in the press for not being Oscar worthy. At CritFlick we let the people decide and not the lords of Hollywood.

We want all of you to join us in writing your reviews and letting the people know what really is good movie making! Feel free to browse the many reviews we already have in our reviews section.

We are constantly adding new features, so keep checking back in! We hope to have a coming soon and currently showing section. We also will add a comments box on your reviews so members can interact with one another.

How To Get Involved?


The first thing you need to do is join us at CritFLick. Just press the join now button above and create a profile using only a username and password!

Once you have joined you can then add your only reviews! It's that simple. Click the add review button and you're on your way to being the next Roger Ebert. All we ask is that you provide a valid url link photo. It will look magnificient above your review!

Here at CritFlick we are ever evolving but really we want to know about all movies. The reviews don't have to be the latest releases. It can be all your old favourites and classics. Have you seen Pulp Fiction 15 times? Let us know and tell us the intricacies of a tale already seen by many. Your point of view might change the movie industry!
